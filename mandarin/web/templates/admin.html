Your personal admin bots. Get status updates, approve content, and manage aelu from Telegram, Discord, or iMessage without opening this dashboard.

What Are These?

OpenClaw bots let you manage aelu from apps you already use. Instead of logging into this admin dashboard, you can send a Telegram message like "how are my users doing?" or "show me the review queue" and get an instant answer. You can also approve or reject AI-generated content right from your phone.

Available Channels

Telegram

Best option. Fast, supports voice messages, works on any device.

Setup:
  1. Message @BotFather on Telegram and create a new bot
  2. Copy the bot token it gives you
  3. Find your Telegram user ID (message @userinfobot)
  4. Set two environment variables: OPENCLAW_TELEGRAM_TOKEN=your-bot-token
    OPENCLAW_TELEGRAM_OWNER_ID=your-user-id
  5. Run: ./run telegram
Discord

Good if you already use Discord. Text only.

Setup:
  1. Go to Discord Developer Portal and create an app
  2. Under "Bot", create a bot and copy its token
  3. Enable "Message Content Intent" in the bot settings
  4. Find your Discord user ID (enable Developer Mode in Discord settings, right-click yourself, Copy ID)
  5. Set two environment variables: OPENCLAW_DISCORD_TOKEN=your-bot-token
    OPENCLAW_DISCORD_OWNER_ID=your-user-id
  6. Run: ./run discord
iMessage

Mac only. Works through the Messages app on your computer.

Setup:
  1. Grant Full Disk Access to your terminal (System Settings → Privacy & Security → Full Disk Access)
  2. Set your phone number or Apple ID: OPENCLAW_IMESSAGE_OWNER_ID=+1234567890
  3. Run: ./run imessage
WhatsApp

Requires a Meta Business account. More involved setup.

Setup:
  1. Create a Meta Business account and WhatsApp Business app
  2. Set environment variables: OPENCLAW_WHATSAPP_TOKEN=your-token
    OPENCLAW_WHATSAPP_PHONE_ID=your-phone-id
    OPENCLAW_WHATSAPP_VERIFY_TOKEN=any-random-string
    OPENCLAW_WHATSAPP_OWNER_NUMBER=+1234567890
  3. Set your webhook URL in Meta dashboard to:
    https://your-domain/api/openclaw/webhook/whatsapp

What You Can Ask

You can type commands or just ask naturally. These all work:

CommandWhat It Does
/statusHow many items are due, your streak, weekly progress
/reviewHow many AI-generated items need your approval
/auditLatest product health check results
/briefingSummary of recent errors and grammar gaps
/errorsRecurring mistake patterns and confusing word pairs

Or just type things like "how am I doing?", "anything to review?", or "what's breaking?" — the bot will figure out what you mean.

On Telegram, you can also send voice messages — the bot will transcribe and respond.
