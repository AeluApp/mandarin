## Budget Recommendations

### Phase 1: Pre-launch (now)
- $0 — build email list organically via Reddit posts and content
- Landing page on GitHub Pages or Netlify (free)
- Formspree free tier for email collection (50 submissions/month)

### Phase 2: Launch (first 30 days)
- $0-100/month — boosted Reddit/Facebook posts only
- Don't run Google Ads yet — need conversion data first
- Focus on organic: 2 Reddit posts/week, 1 blog post/week

### Phase 3: Growth (month 2-6)
- $200-500/month Google Ads on high-intent HSK keywords
- $100-200/month Meta Ads on the "builder story" and "cleanup loop" creatives
- Pause anything with CAC > $30 per paid conversion
- Target: CAC < $15, LTV > $100 (8+ months retention)

### When to STOP paid ads:
- If CAC exceeds $40 per paid user — the unit economics don't work at your price point
- If free-to-paid conversion is below 5% — fix onboarding first
- If churn exceeds 15%/month — fix retention first, ads will just fill a leaky bucket
