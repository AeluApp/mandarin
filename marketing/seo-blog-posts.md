- Soft CTA: "The app I built combines all four methods"

**Intent:** Informational (medium volume, low competition)

- [ ] Images have descriptive alt text
- [ ] Internal links between posts where relevant

- [ ] Page load under 3 seconds (test with PageSpeed Insights)
