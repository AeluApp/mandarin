## STRATEGIC RECOMMENDATIONS

### Priority Tiers

**Tier 1 — Immediate Content Targets (low competition, high relevance):**
- "best spaced repetition app for Chinese" / "Chinese SRS app" / "SRS for Mandarin"
- "Anki alternative Chinese" / "Hack Chinese alternative"
- "HSK 3.0 vocabulary list" / "new HSK 3.0 changes" (time-sensitive, July 2026)
- "adaptive Chinese learning app"
- "how long to pass HSK 3" (your forecasting feature is a unique answer)

**Tier 2 — Blog Content Pillars (medium competition, high volume):**
- "how to pass HSK 1/2/3" series
- "Chinese tone practice" / "Mandarin tone pair drill"
- "best way to learn Chinese characters"
- "spaced repetition for language learning" (educational authority)
- "HSK 3 study plan" / "HSK study schedule"

**Tier 3 — Comparison/Alternative Pages (medium competition, high conversion):**
- "Duolingo Chinese alternative" / "HelloChinese alternative"
- "best Chinese learning app 2026" roundup
- "Anki vs [your app]" (create the comparison before others do)

**Tier 4 — Feature-Specific Long-Tail (very low competition, very high relevance):**
- "interleaved practice Chinese"
- "context-based Chinese learning"
- "data-driven Chinese learning"
- "HSK 3.0 speaking test preparation"

### Timely Opportunity: HSK 3.0

The HSK 3.0 overhaul (launching July 2026) is creating a wave of new searches. Keywords like "new HSK 3.0 vocabulary," "HSK 3.0 changes," and "HSK 3.0 speaking test" are rising fast with low competition. Content published now will have first-mover advantage.

Your app's speaking drills + tone grading are directly relevant to the new HSK 3.0 requirement that HSKK (oral test) is mandatory from level 3 onward.
