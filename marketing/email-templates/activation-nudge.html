Your Aelu account is ready. Here is exactly what happens when you start your first session, so there is no guesswork.

Minutes 0-1: The system gives you a few vocabulary items at the HSK 1 level. Simple characters, pinyin displayed. You are just matching meanings. This is the baseline.

Minutes 1-3: Drill types start to vary. You might get a tone discrimination task (listen to two words, identify which tone you heard) or a cloze task (fill in the missing word in a sentence). The system is testing which skills are strong and which need work.

Minutes 3-5: Based on your first answers, the session adapts. If your tone recognition is shaky, you get more tone drills. If vocabulary is weak, more vocabulary. You do not choose — the system picks what you need.

That is it. Five minutes, and the system knows enough to start building your study plan.

No Chinese knowledge required. No preparation. Just open it and start.
