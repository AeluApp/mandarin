HSK readiness isn't just vocabulary count

You might know 600 words but still fail HSK 3 — because the test measures four distinct skills. Aelu tracks all of them.

词

Vocabulary Recognition

Not just "have I seen this word" — can you recognize it in context at speed?

听

Listening Comprehension

Can you catch the meaning at natural speaking speed, not just slow textbook audio?

读

Reading Fluency

Can you read a passage without stopping to decode every other character?

声

Tone Accuracy

The silent killer on HSK listening — can you distinguish tones in connected speech?
