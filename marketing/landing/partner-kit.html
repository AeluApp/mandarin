Medium (2-3 sentences)
Aelu is an adaptive Chinese learning app with 44 drill types, honest HSK diagnostics, and a graded reader that turns every word you look up into targeted practice. No XP, no streaks, no cartoon mascots. Built for adults who want real data about where they stand.
Long (paragraph)
Aelu tracks your vocabulary, listening, reading, and tone accuracy independently against HSK benchmarks, then adapts your practice session accordingly. The FSRS-based scheduling engine picks from 44 drill types across recognition, production, listening, and speaking modalities. A built-in graded reader lets you read Chinese passages at your level, and every word you tap feeds back into your drill queue automatically. There are no blended averages, no inflated progress bars -- everything is deterministic and instant. HSK 1-2 content is free with no time limit. Full access covers HSK 1-9 content, all drill types, and complete diagnostics — see current pricing on the pricing page.
