## The "Cleanup Loop" Explanation

This is the app's most unique feature. Here's how to explain it simply:

> **The 30-second version:**
> Read a Chinese passage at your level. Tap any word you don't know — you get instant pinyin and definition. Every word you tapped gets added to your drill queue. Your next study session focuses on those exact words. You're not drilling random vocabulary — you're drilling the words YOU actually struggled with.

> **The 10-second version:**
> Every word you look up during reading becomes a drill. Real gaps → real practice.

A: Yes, per FTC guidelines. A simple "I earn a commission if you sign up through my link" is sufficient. Most audiences appreciate the transparency.

**Q: Can I combine the affiliate with a sponsorship payment?**
A: Not currently — we don't have a sponsorship budget. But the affiliate commission is recurring, so active promoters can earn more over time than a one-time sponsorship payment.

**Q: How long does my referral cookie last?**
A: 90 days from when someone clicks your link. If they sign up within 90 days, you get credit for the lifetime of their subscription.

**Q: When do I get paid?**
A: Monthly, once your balance reaches $50. Commissions confirm 30 days after the user's payment (refund window).

**Q: Can I create my own content about the app?**
A: Absolutely. Use whatever format works for your audience. The partner kit has talking points and comparison data, but you're welcome to frame it however you want.

**Q: What if I want to stop being a partner?**
A: Just let us know. Your free account remains active. Existing commissions are paid out.
