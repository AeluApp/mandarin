## Priority Rewrite List

### Tier 1: Formulaic endings (35 passages)
Rewrite endings to prefer: image return, ironic resonance, behavioral reveal, quiet aftertaste.

### Tier 2: Realization crutches (88 passages)
Remove 那一刻我意识到/我突然明白/原来 patterns. Let the scene carry the insight.

### Tier 3: Verbatim-retrieval questions (1,138 questions)
Replace with inference, motive, contrast, emotional reading questions. Priority: HSK 5 (216), HSK 4 (162), HSK 2 (187).

### Tier 4: Thematic deduplication
- Reduce dumplings from 18 to ~8 (keep best, diversify others)
- Reduce library from 21 to ~10
- Reduce "Two Names" from 4 to 2
- Deduplicate bookshop dialogues

### Tier 5: HSK 1 dialogue upgrade
- Replace non-sequitur wrong answers with socially clumsy but relevant options
- Make feedback lines reference specific NPC speech

### Tier 6: Translation polish
- Average EN/ZH ratio is 3.3-3.4x across all levels (consistent)
- Outliers at 4.0+ likely over-explain — check and compress
