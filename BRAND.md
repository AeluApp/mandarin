# 漫 Aelu

Patient Mandarin study. Honest data, no theatrics.

## Identity

**Name:** Aelu
**Mark:** 漫 (màn — drifting, wandering, leisurely)
**Position:** A place for patient language study, not a product that optimizes engagement.

## Voice

- Calm adult. Data-grounded. No praise inflation.
- Every metric defensible. Every number traceable.
- Treats the learner as intelligent and curious.
- Acknowledges difficulty without framing it as failure.
- Feedback directs attention forward (→), not backward (✗).
- Empty states are factual. Errors are actionable. Absence is met with continuity, not guilt.

## Visual

- **Palette:** Mediterranean civic — warm linen, bougainvillea rose, cypress olive, coastal indigo.
- **Typography:** Serif throughout. Cormorant Garamond headings, Source Serif 4 body, Noto Serif SC hanzi.
- **Surface:** Continuous plaster. No card borders. Horizon-line dividers. Radius zero.
- **Motion:** Decelerates into rest. Never bounces. Things arrive, they don't pop.
- **Illustration:** Horizon lines, birds, sun, stars, clouds — tiny SVG punctuation marks at 0.75px stroke.

## Sound

- All UI frequencies above 500Hz (Mandarin tonal F0 range is 75-500Hz).
- Feedback sounds have no pitch center (filtered noise bursts).
- Session sounds use descending intervals (arriving, settling).
- UI sounds should be felt more than heard. Master gain: 0.06-0.08.

## What this is not

- Not a game. No points, no levels, no leaderboards.
- Not a coach. No "Great job!" or "Keep it up!" or "You're on fire!"
- Not a streak machine. Consecutive days are noted, not celebrated.
- Not a dashboard. Information lives in typography and spacing, not in widgets.
